Safer with SCOUT Launches with New Proactive Safety Culture Approach

New Approach in Workplace Safety Receiving Exceptional Reviews from Top-Tier Organizations

Milwaukee WI., July 30, 2024 – Safer with SCOUT (Safety Culture Optimization, Understanding, & Training), the leader in PROACTIVE training related to safety culture development and enhancement, is now available to organizations of all sizes across the United States.

“First, we are very excited to help organizations achieve their organizational safety goals. Moreover, we have created a real paradigm shift in terms of workplace safety culture development,” said Tyler Weston, CEO and Founder of Safer with SCOUT (SwS). For too long workplace safety education and training have focused almost entirely on what to do DURING a chaotic incident (violence), and not on what individuals can do to actively help PREVENT the violence. In our world of increasing risks, we believe organizations need to be proactive, not merely reactive. Organizational safety training must be comprehensive and easily understood, but also contain sufficient depth to be of practical use. For example, pre-employment background checks are a type of risk mitigation, drug testing is another, and another is post-hire monitoring. Each provides information as a snapshot in time, but what is happening between those snapshots? Are your employees trained in situational awareness, continual behavior observation, and do they fully understand the key underlying issues that spawn violence in today’s workplaces…in other words, do they know what to look for? If not, then your organization is exposed to an unnecessary risk,” added Weston.

“I have been conducting in-person training with companies ranging in size from start-ups to FORTUNE 500s for the past twenty years,” said Dan Hanlon, Director of Training. “We quickly realized two things. First, the increased need for what we do, and second, the limitations associated with ‘in-person’ training. These two factors compelled us to craft a LMS (learning management system) that could help educate far greater numbers of people at their own pace and in their own environment. We assembled an amazing team of security experts… I mean, we have truly accomplished people with backgrounds in law enforcement, military, the intelligence community, and federal government safety initiatives who have brought varied and unique perspectives that I do not believe any other educational product offers. And then we integrated this team with the best of current technology to produce the Safer with SCOUT product. People who have reviewed our course modules have told us it is unlike anything they have ever seen…and in a good way.”

ABOUT Safer with SCOUT

Safer with SCOUT (SwS) is the most recent initiative from Noble Six (a part of the Noble Group), which provides a full suite of safety and security protocols, risk mitigation tools such as pre and post-hire background checks/monitoring, drug testing and diagnostics, as well as safety/security training. For more information about Safer with SCOUT, please visit https://www.saferwithscout.com and for more information about NobleSix, https://www.noblesix.us
