About Safer with SCOUT Training

Safer with SCOUT (SwS) training is what's next in building organizations where all stakeholders have not only increased knowledge of today's societal risks and the "foreseeability" of those risks, but more importantly how to respond when a critical incident happens. Our organizational safety training programs are designed to equip your people with the skills and expertise needed to handle threats, violence, and other emergency situations effectively.

LOREM IPSUM

Empowering Organizations

Safer with SCOUT is dedicated to empowering organizations with customized training modules tailored to their specific industry and needs. Our experienced safety instructors have trained (in person) over 2,500 professionals, within FORTUNE 500 companies, with 98% of our clients have provided positive feedback on the effectiveness of our programs.

Safer with SCOUT's learning modules are completely customizable in that there are high-level basic modules that are designed to be easily understood and absorbed by everyone, AND there are accompanying additional modules that offer more detailed (and in some cases advanced) concepts and strategies that, when used in concert with the basic modules, offer a more robust training experience, unlike any other in the marketplace.

Safer with SCOUT also provides dashboard access to management so that individual progress can be monitored... which can be especially helpful in the case of approaching state, local or federal compliance deadlines.
